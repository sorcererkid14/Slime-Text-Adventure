SLIME QUESTS
RULES OF THE GAME: THERE IS AN INVENTORY BOX, A SKILL BOX AND A MAP BOX IN THE RIGHT. THERE YOU CAN SEE THE ITEMS IN YOUR POSSESSTION. THE SKILLS YOU CAN USE AND THE MAP OF THE WHOLE GAME WITH YOUR LOCATION. YOU CAN USE MOVEMENTS SUCH AS 'NORTH', 'EAST', 'SOUTH', AND 'WEST' IF THEY ARE NOT RESTRICTED. YOU CAN ALSO USE OTHER COMMANDS AS WELL, BUT IF THEY ARE NOT VALID OR NOT RECOGNISED BY THE PROGRAMME YOU WILL HAVE TO THINK UP OF AN ALTERNATIVE. INPUT YOUR COMMANDS IN THE GREY BOX BELOW.
HERE ARE SOME COMMANDS THAT MIGHT COME IN HANDY
  • NORTH
  • EAST
  • SOUTH
  • WEST
  • N
  • E
  • S
  • W
  • GET
  • USE
  • EAT
  • YES
  • NO
THERE ARE OTHER COMMANDS AS WELL IN THE GAME THAT ARE NOT LISTED HERE. PLEASE REMEMBER TO USE A HYPHEN BETWEEN THE WORDS. EG: GET MAGIC-STONES.
